Project: Colette

iOS Application

Colette

This is inspired by my day starting again in another country, and I need to measure my budget step by step. When I was looking through options online, I was frightened by the learning curve, even for a simple budgeting app. And all of them are behind a paywall - so I decided to build one myself.

Colette is a simple budget-tracking app that holds me accountable for my food spending. It is named after my favourite bakery in my neighbourhood, where I frequently collect receipts from.

I believe that having an app rather than just Apple Pay and pretending that Apple pays for it is great. Let me know what you think and exciting for more people to use it and colette their receipts.
