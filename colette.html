Project: Colette

iOS App

Demo video

[Link to Source Code] [Back]

This is inspired by my day starting over in the US, and I need to take note my budget dime by dime.

There were no simple budget-recording app on App Store. They are too fancy. Or behind a paywall - so I decided to build one myself.

Colette holds me accountable for my food spending. It is named after my favourite bakery in the neighbourhood, where I frequently collect receipts from.

Let me know what you think and this is how I colette my spendings!
