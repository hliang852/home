What I have been doing since last entry:
Making progress with my [IPO Deep Dive]! While AI agents are doing the scraping and heavy lifting, building AI-powered projects is far from waving one's magic wand - I had to leverage heavily on syntax/"domain expertise" (my days of digging through and reading exchange filing paying off!), as well as a lot of engineering instinct on how to improve efficiency by choosing the path of least ressitance.

>> For instance, instead of repeatedly scraping through a complicated structured non-Html website (takes hours), we are downloading all th files (takes minutes!) before pulling the data with regex. This simple choice saved me hours of debugging scrapers, and frankly it is less memory intensive. It also frees up maybe 20+ hours for me to focus on the analysis and choosing the right tool for the data analysis later.

I have also initating on my project [Agent Daikoku] with the goal of implementing a few engineering concepts that I am excited to try out: RAG, Loop Engineering and Harness Engineering. I had an idea of building something like this for a while, and I will keep you updated with the engineering notebook (linked above). Not only am I experimenting with different engineering concept, I am also expanding my tool box to include Codex now. Fuelled by matcha, time to plan my architecture design.

I have reconnected with learning how to write HTML, reignited my engineering instinsct, and the fun part is always making decisions and calls (unlike my days as a trader!). July will be another month of changes, and I am looking forward to continue to keep this curisoity burning, take good care of myself, and enjoy life.
